Tiya

Tiya is a mysterious archaeological site located just south of Addis Ababa. It features a field of ancient stelae (stone monuments) that mark the burial sites of a lost Ethiopian culture.
The site includes more than 40 stone pillars, many decorated with enigmatic symbols and carvings.

Declared a UNESCO World Heritage Site, Tiya continues to intrigue archaeologists and historians.
Though much about its builders remains unknown, Tiya offers a fascinating glimpse into Ethiopia’s prehistoric past and the diversity of its cultural heritage.
