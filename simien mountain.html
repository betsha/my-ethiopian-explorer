Semien Mountains

The Semien Mountains in northern Ethiopia offer one of Africa’s most breathtaking landscapes.
This UNESCO-listed national park is home to deep valleys, dramatic cliffs, and rare wildlife such as the Gelada baboon, Ethiopian wolf, and Walia ibex.

The region is ideal for trekking, nature photography, and experiencing pristine alpine scenery.
The Simien Mountains are sometimes referred to as the “Roof of Africa” due to their soaring elevations and panoramic views. It’s a paradise for adventurers and nature lovers.
